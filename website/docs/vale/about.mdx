**Vale** is a command-line tool that brings code-like linting to prose. It's
_open-source_, _fast_, and _highly customizable_.

## Your style, our editor

Vale differs from most writing-related software in a significant way: it
doesn't attempt to teach you _how_ to write; it's a tool _for_ writers. In
other words, Vale focuses on allowing its users to create their own collections
of rules (known as [_styles_](styles)) rather providing its own advice.

Here are some examples of how Vale is being used by companies,
organizations, and open-source projects around the world:
